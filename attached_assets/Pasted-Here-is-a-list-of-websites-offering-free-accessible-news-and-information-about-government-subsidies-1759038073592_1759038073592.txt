Here is a list of websites offering free, accessible news and information about government subsidies and grants for farmers managing livestock (especially cattle) and sustainable land management at federal, state, or local levels in the United States:

Federal Programs and News Sources
USDA Farm Service Agency (FSA): Latest updates on federal relief programs, direct payments, and economic assistance for farmers, including livestock and commodity announcements.

Farmers.gov (Farm Bill Portal): Comprehensive news and resources about federal subsidy and grant programs, including current legislation and updates to eligibility and coverage.

USAFacts: Data-driven articles explaining how farm subsidies work by sector, region, and crop, with trend updates and breakdowns of key programs.

State and Local-Level Grant Resources
Minnesota Department of Agriculture (MDA): Timely announcements of livestock investment grants and modernized statewide funding opportunities for cattle and other livestock farmers.

SwoopFunding: Regular listings of state and local grant opportunities, application deadlines, and free funding options tailored to US farmers by state.

Specialized Grants and Sector-Focused Programs
The Livestock Conservancy: Microgrant program for heritage breeds and small livestock operations with transparent criteria, deadlines, and publicly available results.

Farm Action: Advocacy-driven breakdowns of federal farm subsidy sources, news, and eligibility, especially on equity and distribution among livestock producers.

Sustainability, Clean Energy, and Land Management
Inside Climate News & Daily Climate: News analysis covering recent policy shifts affecting sustainable farming and renewable energy funding for farmers, with feedback from sustainable agriculture advocates.

USDA Wildfire Risk Projects: Updates on federal investments for sustainable land management and wildfire risk reduction, including opportunities for livestock producers in at-risk communities.

These sources cover both general farm subsidies and grants for livestock producers, alongside specialized opportunities for sustainable land management and sector-specific news.
