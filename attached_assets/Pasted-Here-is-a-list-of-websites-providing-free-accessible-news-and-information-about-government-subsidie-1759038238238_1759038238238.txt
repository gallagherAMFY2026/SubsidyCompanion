Here is a list of websites providing free, accessible news and information about government subsidies, grants, and support for farmers managing livestock (especially cattle) and sustainable land management in Australia:

National Government Grant and News Sources
Department of Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry (DAFF): Updates on federal grant programs including drought resilience, biosecurity grants, sustainable land and soils funding, and livestock business transition assistance.

DAFF News & Media: Regular news releases, data, and analysis on funding, support programs, and industry trends for Australian farmers.

Sector- and State-Specific Programs
Dairy Australia: Listings and updates on federal and state-level grant programs for dairy and livestock farmers, including drought and disaster support initiatives.

Agriculture Victoria: Information and news on livestock biosecurity grants and disease prevention funding for Victorian farmers.

South Australia PIRSA: Current state grant news and support for drought-affected livestock producers.

ReCFIT Tasmania: Updates and news for low-emissions livestock grant programs and climate-smart farming initiatives, with project and trial details for cattle and other ruminants.

Sustainable Agriculture and Climate-Smart Farming
Landcare Australia/Climate-Smart Agriculture Program: News and details on federal funding to Landcare groups and regional partners supporting sustainable practices, climate resilience, and environmental restoration projects.

Meat & Livestock Australia (MLA): Opportunities for livestock project funding, industry news, and sustainability initiatives.

Technology and Equipment Support
On Farm Connectivity Program: Announcements on rebates and grants for adopting digital farming technology, enhancing productivity and safety for primary producers nationwide.

Recent Funding News Articles
Mirage News & ARENA: Media coverage of climate-smart grants, sustainability projects, and industry transformation efforts impacting cattle producers and others in agriculture.

Argus Media: Industry analysis on grants and financial mechanisms for adopting low-emissions practices in fertilizer and general farming.

These sources offer comprehensive, frequently updated news, grant listings, and program details for Australian farmers seeking support for livestock management and sustainable land practice
