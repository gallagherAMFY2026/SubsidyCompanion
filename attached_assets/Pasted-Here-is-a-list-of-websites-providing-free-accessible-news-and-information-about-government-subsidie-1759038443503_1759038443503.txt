Here is a list of websites providing free, accessible news and information about government subsidies, grants, and support for Chilean farmers managing livestock (primarily cattle) and sustainable land management:

Official Government and Multilateral Sources
Chile Ministry of Agriculture (MINAGRI) & Agricultural Development Institute (INDAP): News on direct support, grants, pest and disease control programs, and sustainable agricultural development, with targeting for small-scale, family, and indigenous farmers.

Inter-American Development Bank (IDB): News releases and grant project updates supporting food security, sustainable growth reforms, and modernization of agricultural services.

USDA Foreign Agricultural Service (FAS): Regular news and export data on Chilean agricultural commodities and policy changes affecting farmers.

Sustainable Land Management, Climate Adaptation, and Biodiversity Finance
Adaptation Fund Chile: Project details and news about grants and capacity building programs for climate resilience in small farming communities, especially in cattle, crop, water, and soil management.

Global Conference on Biodiversity Finance (UNDP/BIOFIN): Updates on Chile’s role in subsidy reform, conservation funding, and access to finance for indigenous and local farmers.

Initiative 20x20: Country-level news and project reports on Chile’s programs for restoration of degraded land, sustainable ranching, and agricultural carbon initiatives.

Energy, Environmental, and Rural Investment News
Global Issues: News stories highlighting small farmer access to solar energy subsidies, including direct examples of irrigation and cooperative benefits.

OilPrice.com: Industry updates on changes to solar and renewable energy subsidies impacting rural farmers.

InvestChile: Information on government grant programs and investment incentives for companies, which can include agricultural and sustainable land projects.

Additional Policy and News Monitoring Outlets
OECD Agricultural Policy Reports: In-depth reviews of Chile’s farm subsidy programs, highlighting types of direct and conditional support for small holders and beef producers.

EIN Presswire – Chile Agriculture Newswire: Ongoing agriculture sector news relevant to grants and program developments.

These sources collectively offer news, program details, official announcements, and policy updates for grant and subsidy opportunities in Chile’s livestock and sustainable land sectors.
